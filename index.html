 Welcome

Koala Tea is a café that has served its customers in Boston, MA, since 2002. We offer pastries, cakes, and coffee, and take special pride in our unique tea blends. If you're hungry, thirsty, or just looking for somewhere to hang out, Koala Tea is the right place for you. (We don't sell any koalas - we're just particularly invested in serving quality teas.)

Note: We are currently hiring - if you're interested in becoming a cashier or barista for us, please contact us here, or drop into our café anytime to speak with one of our staff.
